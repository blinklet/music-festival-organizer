Music Festival Organizer

Designed to support participants and administrators of competitive music festivals.

Whether you're a teacher or student preparing for a musical competition, or a volunteer scheduling sessions and administering registrations, our platform offers the tools you need to ensure a seamless and enriching experience.

Key Features

Our platform offers a range of features to support participants, adjudicators, and administrators throughout the competition process.

Participant Registration

Register as a participant. Parents and teachers may register their children or students. Participants can access their competition schedule.

Adjudicator Information

Adjudicators can track information about the sessions they will adjudicate so it is easy to prepare for sessions and to see changes to the schedule.

Session Scheduling

Administrators can create and manage competition sessions, assign adjudicators, and schedule participant performances.

Teachers and Accompanists

Teachers can see their students' schedules. Accompanists can see the pieces they are accompanying and the schedule each day.

Payment information

Participants can see the cost of all their registrations. Administrators can track payments and provide receipts.

Data Import and Export

Easy-to-use tools for mass registrations and data analysis. Import registrations via spreadsheet and export comprehensive festival data effortlessly.

Simplify Festival Management

Make festival administration a breeze for participants, educators, and administrators alike. Our platform is designed to streamline operations, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for all.
